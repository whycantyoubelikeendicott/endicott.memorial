15 June 2016

I cannot tell you the last time I saw Endi, but I can tell you how absolutely fascinated I am of the life he led. Endicott had the courage I was not born with, although we were family. He was an incredible individual, and my only regret is not having the opportunity to talk to him about his adventures in Australia, as well as the adventures he had experienced throughout his entire life. There are two things helping me to cope with the loss of one of my most adventurous/courageous/brilliant cousins:

  1. Until the very end, you lived life to the fullest.
  2. You are now with Bubba and Pap Pap
    Sending love from the entire family, and constantly thinking about Aunt Lizzie, Uncle Sam, Hannah, Holden, and Simon

14 June 2016

I do not have pictures of Endi. I have never met him. But I know him through the intense love of his sweet mother, our dear friend and colleague, Liz. She shared his accomplishments, his stories of adventure sometimes shaking her head and laughing but always, always lifting him up in love. His soul will continue to shine through all those that he has touched.
~Sara
